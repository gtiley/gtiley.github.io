About Me

I am an evolutionary biologist interested in biodiversity and conservation. This has led down computational and statistical rabbit holes along the way as we all do our best to answer difficult questions. Molecular data and computational methods are the tool I use, but basic research questions are based in natural history.

I believe that science should be inclusive and I am passionate about education in computing, model-based inference, and genomics.
