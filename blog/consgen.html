Networks and Conservation

29 September 2022

The European Conservation Genetics Meeting

It was great to connect with many scientists working on basic and applied research and the policies in conservation genetics in Edinburgh this year. The CONSGEN meeting made an in-person return after a covid hiatus. My goal was to communicate how phylogenetic networks can be useful for understanding evolution, and that I anticipate seeing them more frequently as genomic data is increasingly being used to address conservation genetic questions. My poster is available here.
