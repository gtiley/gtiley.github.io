Teaching

I am moving teaching materials, at least in part, to online from 2021 onward. Covid forced some of us to rethink effective teaching practices. I still think working together on a white board and interactively is best, but I am leveraging GitHub to not only keep materials in a public place for sharing, but also as a collaborative teaching tool for computational biology.

Botany 2023 Workshop Methodological Advances in Reticulate Evolution

A collaborative workshop on phylogenetic network estimation delivered over a full day at the Botany 2023 conference. The organization site is linked here to provide access to materials across instructors.

DBEV Phylogenomics Workshop 2022

Three-day practical phylogenomics workshop hosted by the University of Antananarivo Department of Biology and Plant Ecology. Slides and labs will be accessible here.

Evolutionary Genomics 2021

  2. population genomics
  3. comparative genomics Some material made it to GitHub while I am still thinking of good data hosting solutions for classes aside from on the clusters to make things more accessible. The class can be accessed here.
